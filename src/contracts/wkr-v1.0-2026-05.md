# Limb (b) Worker Agreement

**Version:** wkr-v1.0-2026-05
**Effective from:** Date of countersignature
**Parties:**
- **All Care 4 U Ltd** ("the Company"), trading as SpecialCarer
- **The Worker** ("you", the carer signing below)

---

## 1. Status

You are engaged as a **Limb (b) worker** under Section 230(3)(b) of the Employment Rights Act 1996. You are not an employee and you are not self-employed for the purposes of Channel B shifts dispatched under this Agreement.

## 2. Channel B shifts

The Company may offer you shifts ("Channel B Shifts") sourced from organisational clients of All Care 4 U. You are free to accept or decline any Channel B Shift offered. There is no mutuality of obligation between offers — the Company is not required to offer you work and you are not required to accept any offer.

## 3. Pay

For each Channel B Shift you accept and complete, the Company will pay you the rate notified at the time of the offer, which will not be less than the National Living Wage applicable to you.

Pay is processed weekly via PAYE. You will receive a payslip itemising hours, rate, tax, National Insurance and pension contributions.

## 4. Holiday pay

You are entitled to statutory holiday pay (currently 12.07% of gross pay) in accordance with the Working Time Regulations 1998. Holiday pay accrues on every Channel B Shift completed and is paid weekly alongside basic pay (rolled-up holiday pay, permitted for irregular-hours workers).

## 5. Marketplace shifts unaffected

This Agreement does **not** affect your status as a self-employed marketplace carer for any shifts you arrange directly with families through SpecialCarer's marketplace ("Channel A"). For Channel A you remain self-employed and responsible for your own tax and National Insurance.

## 6. Compliance obligations

To remain eligible for Channel B Shifts you must maintain:

- A valid Enhanced DBS with Children's & Adults' Barred Lists check, issued within the last 12 months.
- A current Right to Work confirmation with no upcoming expiry within 60 days.
- Completion of the three mandatory training courses: Safeguarding Adults, Manual Handling, and Infection Control.

If any of the above lapses, you will be paused from Channel B until cleared. Pausing does not terminate this Agreement.

## 7. Conduct

While on a Channel B Shift you act for and on behalf of the Company. You will:

- Follow the care plan provided by the receiving organisation.
- Treat service users with dignity and respect.
- Report safeguarding concerns to the Company's Safeguarding Lead immediately.
- Not solicit Channel A bookings from the receiving organisation's service users.

## 8. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time on written notice. Termination ends your eligibility for Channel B Shifts but does not affect any Channel A activity.

## 9. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
