## ▸ Description (4000 chars max)

```
Special Carer is the on-demand marketplace for trusted, verified caregivers — for elderly loved ones, children, and people with special needs.

Built for families who want peace of mind, and caregivers who deserve to be paid fairly. Available across the United Kingdom and the United States.

WHY FAMILIES CHOOSE SPECIAL CARER

• Every caregiver is identity-verified and background-checked (Enhanced DBS in the UK; Checkr national + county in the US).
• Live shift tracking with real-time location updates while the carer is on shift.
• Escrow payments via Stripe — your card is authorised when you book and only charged after the shift ends, with a 24-hour cool-off window for disputes.
• In-app messaging per booking. SOS button during active shifts.
• Transparent pricing — caregivers' displayed rate is the all-in cost (carer rate + 30% service fee shown clearly at checkout).
• 24/7 trust & safety support.

WHY CAREGIVERS CHOOSE SPECIAL CARER

• Keep 70% of every shift — set your own hourly rate.
• No subscription fees, no listing fees.
• Free Enhanced DBS (UK) or Checkr (US) screening — we cover the cost.
• Same-day payouts via Stripe (where your bank supports it).
• Pick the shifts that work for you.
• Free training resources and a top-rated programme for higher visibility.

CARE FORMATS

Visiting carers — booked by the hour, from £18/hour in the UK and $25/hour in the US. Minimum 1 hour for repeat bookings, 3 hours for a first booking with a new carer.

Live-in carers — booked by the week. Indicative ranges £700–£900/week (UK) and $1,000–$1,400/week (US), depending on complexity of care and whether nights are sleeping or waking.

WHO IT'S FOR

• Families needing childcare, after-school care, or holiday cover.
• Adult children arranging support for elderly parents — companionship, personal care, dementia support.
• Parents of children with special needs looking for experienced support.
• New parents needing postnatal help.
• Anyone needing short-notice or recurring care from a vetted local professional.

PRIVACY AND TRUST

Special Carer never tracks you across other apps or websites. Live location is shared only during an active booking, and stops when the shift ends. Payments are handled by Stripe — we never see or store your card details. Background checks are run by uCheck (UK) and Checkr (US), both regulated providers.

Read our privacy policy at https://www.specialcarer.com/privacy and our terms at https://www.specialcarer.com/terms.

ABOUT US

Special Carer is operated by All Care 4 U Group Ltd, a UK company building a fairer care economy.

Questions? Email office@allcare4u.co.uk or visit https://www.specialcarer.com/contact.
```

### Why these keywords

- `caregiver` — US-leaning generic
- `babysitter` — high US search volume for childcare
- `childcare` — UK + US generic, parent-facing
- `eldercare` — captures the elderly-care vertical
- `nanny` — household-staff search bucket
- `carer` — UK-specific spelling (different from `caregiver`)
- `DBS` — UK-specific trust signal (anyone searching "DBS babysitter" or "DBS childminder" finds us)
- `childminder` — UK-specific (Ofsted regulated category)
- `caretaker` — alt UK term for live-in
- `sitter` — short-form catch-all
- `respite` — high-intent for special-needs and dementia families

Words ALREADY in the app name/subtitle do **not** need to go here — Apple already indexes those:
- "special carer" (name)
- "verified care on demand" (subtitle)

## ▸ Sequence to follow in App Store Connect

When you're ready to submit (NOT for the first TestFlight — only for App Store), do this in order:

1. **Pricing & Availability** → Free, GB + US only
2. **App Information** → category, content rights, age rating
3. **App Privacy** → use `APP_PRIVACY_QUESTIONNAIRE.md`
4. **Version** (1.0) → paste subtitle, promotional text, description, keywords from above
5. **Screenshots** → upload (we'll generate)
6. **Build** → select the latest TestFlight build
7. **App Review Information** → use `APP_REVIEW_NOTES.md`
8. **Version Release** → "Manually release this version" (recommended for first launch — gives you control of the go-live moment)
9. Click **Add for Review** → **Submit to App Review**

Then wait. Apple's median review time is 24–48 hours for new apps; first-time submissions sometimes take 3–5 days.
